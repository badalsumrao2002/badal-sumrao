Our Services

Home Car Rental offers a comprehensive range of taxi services to meet all your travel needs. We are committed to providing high-quality service, no matter where you're going.

One-Way Taxi

Perfect for when you just need to get from one city to another, like from Nashik to Mumbai or Pune. You pay only for the distance you travel.

Round-Trip Taxi

Ideal for short getaways or business trips where you need a cab for the return journey as well. Our round-trip packages are cost-effective and convenient.

Airport Transfers

We provide timely and reliable pickups and drops for Mumbai and Pune airports. Start or end your journey without any stress.

Local Sightseeing

Explore the best of Nashik and nearby tourist spots with our flexible hourly and day-based packages. Our drivers act as your local guide.

Corporate Travel

We offer professional and punctual cab services for business meetings, corporate events, and daily travel for executives.

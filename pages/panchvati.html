Panchvati Local Sightseeing Taxi

Discover the spiritual heart of Nashik by visiting Panchvati. Associated with the epic Ramayana, this sacred area is a must-visit. Our local sightseeing taxi packages make it easy to explore all the important sites.

Trip Details

  • Distance from Nashik: Panchvati is located within Nashik city itself.
  • Things to See:
    • Kalaram Temple: An ancient and beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Rama.
    • Sita Gufa (Sita's Cave): The cave where Sita is believed to have stayed.
    • Tapovan: A serene place where sages once meditated.
    • Ramkund: The sacred bathing ghat on the Godavari river.

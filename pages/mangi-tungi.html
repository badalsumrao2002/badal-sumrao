Nashik to Mangi-Tungi Taxi Service

Visit the historic Jain pilgrimage site of Mangi-Tungi with a comfortable taxi from Nashik. This twin-pinnacled peak holds great significance for the Jain community.

Trip Details

  • Distance from Nashik: Approximately 125 km.
  • Things to See: The world's tallest Jain statue, the Statue of Ahimsa, representing the first Jain Tirthankara, Rishabhanatha. There are also numerous temples on the hills.
  • Estimated Fare: Contact us for customized packages for your pilgrimage to Mangi-Tungi.

A Comfortable Pilgrimage

  • Safe Travel: Our priority is to provide a safe and smooth ride for all pilgrims.
  • Ample Space: We have vehicles to accommodate groups and families comfortably.
  • Flexible Timings: We can adjust the travel schedule to suit your darshan and prayer timings.

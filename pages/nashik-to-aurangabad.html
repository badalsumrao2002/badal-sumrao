Explore Aurangabad's Treasures

Aurangabad, the city of gates, is a hub of historical monuments. With our cab service, you can comfortably visit world-famous sites:

  • Ellora Caves: A UNESCO World Heritage site, these rock-cut caves are a magnificent example of Indian art and architecture.
  • Bibi ka Maqbara: Often called the 'Tomb of the Lady', this beautiful mausoleum is a stunning replica of the Taj Mahal.
  • Daulatabad Fort: A formidable hill fort that offers panoramic views and a glimpse into medieval warfare.
